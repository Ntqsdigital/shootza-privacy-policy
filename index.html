Privacy Policy for Shootza

Last updated: July 7, 2026

Shootza ("we", "our", "us") operates the Shootza mobile application (the "App"), a platform that connects clients with photography and videography creators for bookings. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: name, email address, phone number, and password (or OTP verification) when you register or log in.
  • Profile information: profile photos, portfolio/work samples (for creators), and other details you choose to add to your profile.
  • Booking information: event details, booking dates, pricing, and communication related to bookings between clients and creators.
  • Location data: with your permission, we access your device's location to help match clients with nearby creators and to display relevant event locations.
  • Payment information: payments are processed through third-party payment gateways (such as Razorpay or Stripe). We do not store your full card or bank details on our servers — these are handled directly by the payment provider.
  • Usage data: basic technical information such as device type and app usage patterns to help us improve the App.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account (client or creator)
  • To facilitate bookings between clients and creators
  • To process payments securely through our payment partners
  • To send booking confirmations, OTPs, and important notifications
  • To improve app features, fix bugs, and enhance user experience
  • To show relevant creators or events based on your location

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only:

  • Between clients and creators as necessary to complete a booking (e.g., contact details, event requirements)
  • With third-party payment gateways to process transactions
  • When required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Choices

  • You can update or delete your profile information at any time within the App.
  • You can disable location permissions through your device settings, though this may limit certain features.
  • You can request account deletion by contacting us at the email below.

6. Children's Privacy

Shootza is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
